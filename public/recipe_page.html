Orange Chicken
Total Time: 1 hr 20 mins
Adapted from https://www.modernhoney.com/chinese-orange-chicken/
Poultry
Ingredients:
  • -Chicken:
    • 3 chicken breasts
    • 1 egg - whisked
    • 1/2 cup Corstarch
    • 1/2 cup Flour
    • Salt
  • -Orange Chicken Sauce:
    • 1 cup Orange Juice
    • 2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce

    Preperation

    1. Make orange sauce
    2. Make chicken

    Cooking

    1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit
    2. Leave chicken in oven for 10 minutes
    3. Take chicken out and enjoy with orange sauce
    4. Preheat oven to 500 degrees Fahrenheit
